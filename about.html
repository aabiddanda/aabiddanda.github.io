Research

I am currently a senior at Cornell University studying Computer Science. My interests are in taking methods from Machine Learning, Graph Theory, and Statistics and applying them to large-scale genomic problems.

Currently I am working in Alon Keinan's Lab studying the effects of sex-biased admixture and developing software to analyze the X-chromosome in Genome-Wide association studies.
